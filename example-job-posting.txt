Software Engineer — Relay (Austin, TX)

Relay is a fintech startup building the banking platform for small businesses.
We're looking for a mid-level software engineer to join our product team and
help scale our core backend infrastructure.

What you'll do:
- Design and build APIs that power Relay's web and mobile products
- Improve reliability and performance of our Node.js backend services
- Collaborate with product and design to ship features end to end
- Participate in code review and help raise the bar for engineering quality
- Contribute to architectural decisions as we scale from thousands to millions of users

What we're looking for:
- 2–5 years of software engineering experience
- Strong foundation in backend development (Node.js preferred)
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL a plus)
- Comfortable working across the stack when needed
- Someone who takes ownership and communicates clearly

Nice to have:
- Experience with microservices or distributed systems
- Familiarity with AWS or similar cloud platforms
- Prior work at a startup or high-growth company

We're a small team that moves fast. You'll have real ownership over what you build.
